# Quickstart: History Page Entry Redesign

**Branch**: `012-history-entry-design` | **Date**: 2026-05-10

## What Changed

The Workout History page has been redesigned to display sessions as a clean, flat list. Each entry now shows:

- **Workout name** — bold, at a glance
- **Date and time** — directly below the name, in a quieter style (e.g., "10 May 2026 · 2:30 PM")

The old "Today / Yesterday / 3 Days Ago" section headers are gone.

---

## Viewing Your History

1. Open the app and navigate to **History** in the sidebar.
2. Your completed workouts appear in a flat list, newest first.
3. Each entry shows the workout name and when you completed it.
4. Tap or click any entry to expand it and see the exercises you logged.
5. Tap or click again to collapse.

---

## What Stays the Same

- Exercise details (weight, effort, notes) are still viewable by expanding an entry.
- The empty state message appears when no sessions have been logged yet.
- Loading and error states behave exactly as before.
